Since it's hard for Taylor to get her masters back, she has instead decided to re-record her albums. So far, she has re-recorded her second album, Fearless. The album also included unreleased songs. You will know a song is the remade version when it includes the phrase "Taylor's Version" in the title. The release of Red (Taylor's Version) is scheduled to come out in November.



Why is it important to stream "Taylor's Version" of songs?

Re-recording her songs is her way of reclaiming her music. And this time around, she will be able to have full control over how the music is produced. She hopes that her re-recorded versions will be sought out instead of the original. Buying and streaming her new versions will help support her and rally more attention around the remade songs.
